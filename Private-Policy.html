Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Coffee House. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or book our rooms.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and phone number when you fill out a booking form.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, such as your IP address and browser type.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To process and confirm your room or table reservations.
  • To improve our customer service and website functionality.
  • To send promotional emails about new coffee blends or special events (only with your consent).

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, to ask for corrections, or to request the deletion of your data at any time. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.
