Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common queries about our café and services.

Do you offer home delivery services?
+

Yes, we provide fresh delivery across the city. You can place your order directly through our website or via our partner delivery apps.

What kind of coffee beans do you use?
+

We use premium 100% Arabica beans, ethically sourced from the best high-altitude farms in Ethiopia and Brazil, roasted daily for maximum freshness.

How can I book a Private or Meeting Room?
+

You can book your preferred space by visiting our "Rooms" section and filling out the reservation form, or by calling our support line for instant booking.

Are there any sugar-free or vegan options?
+

Absolutely! We offer Stevia as a natural sweetener and provide various dairy-free milk alternatives like Almond and Oat milk for our vegan customers.

What are your opening and closing hours?
+

Our café is open from 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM on weekdays. On weekends (Friday-Sunday), we stay open until 1:00 AM to serve your late-night cravings.

Can I host a small event at your café?
+

Yes, we have a specialized Event Room perfect for birthday parties, workshops, and corporate meetings. Contact our team for customized décor and catering packages.
